MISSION

Here in this website we are providing a platform for helping students to find study centres or reading libraries in different cities to continue their studies in flow. Our website will be very useful for candidates who have migrated to new unknown city. This website will provide detailed information of nearby libraries of almost every city of country and all the facilities available for students like number of seats available and monthly fee among many more.

VISION

It is our heartly intention to ease the problems of students in new places to fine study centres. Along with that this site will add additional benefit of providing many other information like the seats available, seating facility, availability of newspapers and magazines for competitive exams, personal mentorship, location, and price per hour per day , etc.
